About Us

Kurokoffee is derived from Japanese words "kuro", which means black, and "kōhī", the Katakana for coffee. From farm to cup. Most of our coffee beans are sourced from our plantation in West Java, Indonesia. Going through careful selection processes, we ensure only the best beans go into your cup. We plant, process and roast our coffee in-house.

Our main coffee beans are sourced from our private plantation in Maribaya region of West Java, Indonesia. We also source coffee beans from other plantation around Indonesia and overseas.
